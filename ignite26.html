ABOUT IGNITE 2026

"Sports teach us resilience, discipline, and unity; values that extend far beyond the field."

After the remarkable success of its previous editions, IGNITE 2026 marks yet another chapter in the legacy of IGDTUW's Annual Sports Fest. Organized by Synergy, the official sports club, this two-day sporting celebration brings together talented athletes from across colleges. We are honored to welcome Sonia Chahal (an accomplished Indian boxer and international medalist) as our esteemed guest. With the continued guidance of Dr. Dinesh Ganotra, IGNITE 2026 promises passion, sportsmanship, and unforgettable moments.
