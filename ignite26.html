IGNITE-26

IGNITE 2026 is the annual celebration of sportsmanship, strength, teamwork, and discipline at IGDTUW. Teams from colleges across India will compete in track events, indoor games, and team sports. The event focuses on fair competition, smooth coordination, and high-energy participation.
